Bio

I work on AI and search at Snowflake Inc.. I study accessible information retrieval, with a focus on explainability, reasoning, and multilingual search.

I earned my Ph.D. from Manning CICS at UMass Amherst, where I was part of the Center for Intelligent Information Retrieval (CIIR) and advised by James Allan and Negin Rahimi.
